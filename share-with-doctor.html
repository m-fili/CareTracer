How to share this with your doctor

1
Send the link to your doctor Text, email, print, or hand-deliver. The link by itself does not open the record.
2
Share the six-digit code separately Call, read aloud, or text via a different channel. Keeping the two halves apart is what makes the share secure.
3
Your doctor opens the link and enters the code They see a read-only snapshot of your record for the duration of the share window.

What your doctor will see

Your full unified record, read-only, for the duration of the share.
